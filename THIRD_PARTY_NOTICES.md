# Third-Party Notices

## Rider-Waite-Smith Tarot card images

The tarot card face images in `static/tarot/cards/` are based on the original
Rider-Waite-Smith tarot deck illustrations by Pamela Colman Smith, first
published in 1909/1910.

Source: Wikimedia Commons file pages for the `RWS_Tarot_*`, `Wands*`, `Cups*`,
`Swords*`, and `Pents*` images.

License/status: Wikimedia Commons marks these original card face images as
public domain in the United States. Avoid replacing them with modern recolored,
restored, boxed, or commercial deck images unless a separate license permits it.

The card back image at `static/tarot/card-back.svg` is an original project
asset created for this application.
